# Microsoft Intune Device Management

## Objective

Demonstrate how Microsoft Intune is used to securely manage corporate devices, enforce security policies, deploy applications, and protect company data across Windows devices.

---

## Business Scenario

The company recently adopted a hybrid work environment where employees work both in the office and remotely. As new laptops are issued to staff, the IT department must ensure every device complies with the company's security standards before employees gain access to Microsoft 365 resources.

---

## Business Requirement 

- Enroll newly issued corporate laptops
- Apply device compliance policies
- Deploy standard business applications
- Configure device security settings
- Restrict access from non-compliant devices
- Retire devices belonging to departing employees

This reduces manual device configuration while ensuring all company devices remain secure and compliant.
